Product / variants sales price history on each product

This module will help you to track the history of each product sale price on each sales order to decide your future planning to decide the sale price of the product based on demand and the price sold history.

There are 3 scenarios to see price history based on...
1. Order confirm : It will show product price history on each product for the order confirm only

2. Done / Locked : It will show product price history on each product for the only Done / Lock

3. Both : It will show all orders history on each product

Below is the product template and it will show the sales price history with customer name, sales person who sold in which price, Sales order number, order date, product name with qty sold and the unit price of that product
