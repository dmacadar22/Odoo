POS Age Restriction on Product

With the use of this addon, the seller can restrict the sell of products to under age.

Product Form

Sellers can set age restriction on every product.

POS Purchase Time Notification for Age Restricted Products

If the customer doesn't seems to be under age then using the "Yes" button the products will be directly added into the cart else a customer age verification popup will arrive to enter birthdate of customer to auto-compute the year.

If the customer's age seems to be eligible to buy the product then the product will be added in cart and a green signal will be provided.

If you customer's age doesn't seems to be eligible to buy the product then the product will not be allowed to buy and a red signal will be provided.

Custom message can be set for eligible and not eligible popup's.
